Ground Game

Intro

In this blog post, I look into the “Ground Game,” or the non-ad components to political US House campaigns. Primarily, I focus on Extension #2 whether expert predictions (blog post 4) and ad spending (blog post 5) affect turnout at the district level. I end with an update to my model and a further look into my specific district, OH-01.
